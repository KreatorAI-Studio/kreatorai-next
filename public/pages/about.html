Our Story

Why We Built This

KreatorAI was born from frustration. As creators ourselves, we watched talented people spend 80% of their time on logistics — writing scripts, hunting for hashtags, crafting captions, editing videos — instead of doing what they love: creating.

We built the tool we wished existed. One that understands the modern creator's context, handles the full production pipeline with AI, and fits into a mobile-first workflow. No bloated dashboards. No generic outputs. Just AI that actually gets it.

KreatorAI is built to be the one place creators and brands manage their entire social media presence — and we're just getting started.

The Developer Who Became a Creator Problem-Solver

Neil Surjiani started KreatorAI with a simple belief: creators are some of the most creative people on the planet — they just need better tools. He combined his background in Flutter development and a deep passion for the creator economy to build the platform he wished existed.

An engineer at heart, Neil personally built every feature in the current app — from the Flutter frontend to the Python AI backend. He talks to creators daily, ships updates weekly, and is relentlessly focused on making content creation faster and more joyful for every creator on the platform.

When he's not building KreatorAI, Neil is documenting the journey of building in public and helping other indie developers ship their first product.
