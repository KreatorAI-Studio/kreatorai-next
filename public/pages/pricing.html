Pricing Questions
Is the free plan really free?
Completely free. No credit card required. 3 generations per day, forever — no trial expiry. All text tools are always free, with 1 connected platform and 1 workspace.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major international cards via Stripe. Local payment methods vary by region and are added regularly.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your billing cycle.
What's the difference between Pro and Business?
Pro is built for a single creator on one workspace with up to 4 connected platforms. Business adds unlimited platform connections and up to 5 workspaces, ideal for managing multiple brands or clients.
